ANNEX 31
High priority goods according to Article 14f
This annex refers to the 'List of Common High Priority Items' drawn up by the EU in cooperation with the United States, the United Kingdom and Japan, and nominally includes prohibited goods 
that are used in Russian military systems or are essential for developing, manufacturing or deploying such systems.
